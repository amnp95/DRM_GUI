Equation Numberers
==================

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 1
   :caption: Available Numbering Methods

   plain
   rcm
   amd
   parallelrcm

Numberers in OpenSees determine how equation numbers are assigned to degrees of freedom (DOFs) in the structural model. The choice of numbering scheme can significantly impact the computational efficiency of the analysis by affecting matrix bandwidth and fill-in during factorization.
